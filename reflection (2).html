Reflection on This Website

My Reflection

Working on this project has been a challenging yet rewarding experience. I’ve struggled with HTML because my short-term memory often makes it hard to retain lessons I’ve learned, and balancing both HTML and CSS feels overwhelming at times. My mind is more accustomed to programming languages like Java and C++, so switching between these languages has been an adjustment. To overcome these challenges, I’ve relied on YouTube tutorials to review and reinforce my understanding, applying concepts step by step in my work. Although it has taken considerable time and effort, I remain committed to improving. This experience reminded me of the importance of persistence and continuous learning. Every challenge I face is an opportunity to grow, and I am determined to keep trying my best to excel in my major.
